USBgear’s new mini USB to RS-232 Plug-in Adapter is designed to make serial port expansion quick and simple.


Drivers are available for: Windows 7, Windows 8, Windows 8.1, Windows 10


Connecting to a USB port on your Tablet PC, notebook or PC, this USB-COM Plug-in adapter instantly adds RS-232 serial communication port to your system.


The product is the smallest and most flexible USB to RS-232 Plug-in Adapter. It is featured with the biggest internal FIFO, 128 byte transmit and 384 byte receive FIFO, for high-speed data transfer rate.


Plugging the mini USB-COM Plug-in Adapter to the USB 2.0 or 1.1 port, the adapter is automatically detected and installed. There are no IRQ & COM port conflicts, since the port doesn’t need any additional IRQ, DMA, memory and I/O port as resources on the system.
